How far would other cities' rail costs carry LA's D Line?

This comparison uses Los Angeles Metro's D Line extension as the baseline and asks how far the same overall project budget would go if it were built at the cost per mile seen in other North American cities. The left rail shows each city's cost-per-mile multiple versus LA, and the city list on the right moves the purple extension to the last station that budget would reach.
